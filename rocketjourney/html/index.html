WELCOME CAPTAIN!

You have been chosen to visit the nearest planet to our spaceship to pick up more energy crystals for our ship.

Your rocket has primative engines, but we'll upgrade it every time you come back to the ship. Later expeditions will become progressively longer.

Along your journey, be sure to look out for the following things:

  • Energy Crystals: This is what you're here for! Try to get as many as you can in the time you have.
  • Rocks: Be careful of these! If your ship hits too many rocks it will force a return back to our ship for repairs. Your ship will still fly while your ship has more than -5 shields.
  • Shield Rechargers: These will improve the strength of your shields, up to a maximum of 5! You'll lose a shield point for every rock you hit (which can happen at higher speeds!)

Enjoy the journey!
